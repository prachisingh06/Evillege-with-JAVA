Scholarship

The rate of scholarship towards tuition fee for General Degree courses is Rs.30,000 per annum, for Engineering course Rs.1.25 lakh per annum and for medical studies Rs.3.0 lakh per annum. Fixed Maintenance allowance of Rs. 1.0 lakh per annum is provided to all students under the scheme.

PM Scholarship Yojana 2023: PM Scholarship Yojana 2023 has done justice to the candidates who are in the blood relation of a person serving or have served defense forces. Except from January to April, it is open all year. The PM Scholarship Yojana 2023 applicant will get monthly rewards and privileges worth a certain amount. Prime Minister Narendra Modi announced a new programe called the PM Scholarship Yojana 2023. The mai aim of these different scholarships is to link particular sectors of our society through Education + Literacy = Growth.
