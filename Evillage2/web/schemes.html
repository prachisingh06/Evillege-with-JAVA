Under Sarkari Yojana, the Government of India is extending various types of welfare schemes to all eligible beneficiaries of the country. After becoming pradhan Mantri in the year 2014, Honorable pradhan Mantri Shri Narendra Modi has launched many schemes in the interest of the country. Today through this article, we will provide you with the main information about the main schemes launched by the pradhan Mantri like important documents, benefits, important dates, registration process, user guidelines and official website.


Different ministries of the government of India formulate various development schemes not to raise the profit but to maximise the welfare of the people. Deen Dayal Upadhyay Grameen Kaushal Yojna

This is a placement linked skill development scheme for rural poor youth.It was launched
by on 25 September 2014 by Union Ministers Nitin Gadkari and Venkaiah Naidu on
the occasion of 98th birth anniversary of Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya. It aims to
target youth, under the age group of 15–35 years A total of 52000 candidates
have been skilled under this programme till 2014-15.

Swachchh Bharat Mission

The Prime Minister launched Swachh Bharat Mission on the birth an
of Mahatma Gandhi on 2nd October, 2014 The concept of Swachh Bharat
Abhiyan is to pave access for every person to sanitation facilities
including toilets, solid and liquid waste disposal systems, village
cleanliness and safe and adequate drinking water supply.The
programme is to be implemented by Ministry
of Drinking Water
and Sanitation.An action plan has been drawn up for Swachh Bharat
to become a reality by 2019, the 150th birth anniversary of Mahatma
Gandhi.The Mission aims to triple the growth percentage of
toilet from present 3% to 10% by 2019.

Pradhan Mantri Kusum Yojana

Solar energy powered solar pumps will be provided to farmers through the Pradhan
Mantri Kusum Yojana. The scheme has been extended by the government till
2023 under which the target of achieving a capacity of 30.8 GW has been set.
A budget of Rs 34,035 crore has been set by the government for the successful
implementation of the Pradhan Mantri Kusum Yojana. In addition to solar pumps
through the Pradhan Mantri Kusum Yojana, grid connected solar power and other
privatized power systems will also be provided to the farmers. So that the income
of the farmers will increase

Ayushman Sahakar Yojana

Hospitals, health care for education, establishment of infrastructure,
modernization, expansion, repair, renovation will be done in the field
of health through Ayushman cooperative scheme. Under this scheme,
loan of 10 thousand crores will be distributed to cooperative
societies.
So that the Sehkari committees will establish health facilities.
Government medical sector will be strengthened through Ayushman
cooperative scheme and permission will also be given to open
medical colleges and hospitals under this scheme.
